Item b

Find the gcd's and lcm's of the following pairs of numbers. \
\(a\) \(b\) \(\gcd{a}{b}\) \(\lcm{a}{b}\)
110 273
105 42
168 189
\hint{For such small numbers you can just find their prime factorizations and use that, although it might be useful to practice your understanding of the Euclidean algorithm by tracing through it to find the gcd's and then using the formula \begin{equation*} \lcm (a,b) = \frac{ab}{\gcd (a,b).} \end{equation*} }
