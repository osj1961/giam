The traditional view seems to be that \begin{equation*} \Naturals = \{1, 2, 3, 4, \ldots \} \end{equation*} i.e. that the naturals don't include 0. My personal preference would be to make the other choice (i.e. to include \(0\) in the natural numbers), but for the moment, let's be traditionalists.
