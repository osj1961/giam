True or false? \begin{equation*} \left\lfloor \frac{n}{d}\right\rfloor \lt \left\lceil \frac{n}{d}\right\rceil \end{equation*} for all integers \(n\) and \(d>0\). Support your claim. \hint{You have to try a bunch of examples. You should try to make sure the examples you try cover all the possibilities. The pairs that provide counterexamples (i.e. show the statement is false in general) are relatively sparse, so be systematic.}
