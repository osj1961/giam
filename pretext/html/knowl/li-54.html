Item h

Use the tables from the previous problem to make the following conversions.

  1. Convert \(757_8\) to binary.

  2. Convert \(1007_8\) to hexadecimal.

  3. Convert \(100101010110_2\) to octal.

  4. Convert \(1111101000110101_2\) to hexadecimal.

  5. Convert \(FEED_{16}\) to binary.

  6. Convert \(FFFFFF_{16}\) to octal.

\hint{Answers for the first three: \begin{equation*} 757_8 = 111 101 111_2 \end{equation*}\begin{equation*} 1007_8 = 001 000 000 111_2 = 0010 0000 0111_2 = 207_{16} \end{equation*}\begin{equation*} 100 101 010 110_2 = 4526_8 \end{equation*} }
