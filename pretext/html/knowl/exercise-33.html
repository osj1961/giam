Exercise 4.2.1

Let \(A = \left\{ 1, 2, \{ 1 \}, \{ a, b \} \right\}\). Which of the following are true?

i) \(\{ a, b \} \subseteq A\). vi) \(\{ 1 \} \subseteq A\).
ii) \(\{ a, b \} \in A\). vii) \(\{ 1 \} \in A\).
iii) \(a \in A\). viii) \(\{ 2 \} \in A\).
iv) \(1 \in A\). ix) \(\{ 2 \} \subseteq A\).
v) \(1 \subseteq A\). x) \(\{\{1\}\} \subseteq A\).
