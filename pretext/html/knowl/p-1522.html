Consider the collection of all subsets of size \(k\) taken from \(N = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, n\}\) in which one of the elements has been marked to distinguish it from the others in some way. 1  For example, a committee of \(k\) individuals one of whom has been chosen as chairperson, is an example of the kind of entity we are discussing.
