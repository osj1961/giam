Define a relation \(\relA\) on the set of all words by \begin{equation*} w_1 \relA w_2 \iff w_1 \mbox{ is an anagram of } w_2. \end{equation*} Show that \(\relA\) is an equivalence relation. (Words are anagrams if the letters of one can be re-arranged to form the other. For example, `ART' and `RAT' are anagrams.)
