Examples:

  1. \(P(x)\) = “\(2^{2^x}+1\) is a prime.”

  2. \(Q(x,y)\) = “\(x\) is prime or \(y\) is a divisor of \(x\).”

  3. \(L(f,c,l)\) = “The function \(f\) has limit \(l\) at \(c\), if and only if, for every positive number \(\epsilon\), there is a positive number \(\delta\) such that whenever \(|x-c| \lt \delta\) it follows that \(|f(x)-l| \lt \epsilon\).”
