Adding \((-1)^{k} (k+1)^2\) to both sides gives \begin{gather*} \sum_{i=1}^{k+1} (-1)^{i-1} i^2 \;= \; (-1)^{k-1} \frac{k(k+1)}{2} + (-1)^{k} (k+1)^2\\ \;= \; (-1)^{k-1} \frac{k(k+1)}{2} - (-1)^{k-1} (k+1)^2\\ \;= \; (-1)^{k-1} \left( \frac{k(k+1)}{2} - \frac{2(k+1)^2}{2} \right)\\ \;= \; (-1)^{k} \left( \frac{2(k+1)^2}{2} - \frac{k(k+1)}{2} \right)\\ \;= \; (-1)^{k} \frac{(k+1)(2(k+1)-k)}{2}\\ \;= \; (-1)^{k} \frac{(k+1)(k+2)}{2} \end{gather*}
