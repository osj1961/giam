Thus \begin{gather*} \sum_{k=1}^{m+1} k^3 \;= \; \left( \frac{m^2(m+1)^2}{4} \right) + \frac{4(m+1)^3}{4}\\ \;= \; \left( \frac{m^2(m+1)^2 + 4 (m+1)^3}{4} \right)\\ \; = \; \left( \frac{(m+1)^2 (m^2 + 4(m+1))}{4} \right)\\ \; = \; \left( \frac{(m+1)^2 (m^2 + 4m +4)}{4} \right)\\ \; = \; \left( \frac{(m+1)^2 (m+2)^2}{4} \right)\\ \; = \; \left( \frac{(m+1)(m+2)}{2} \right)^2 \end{gather*}
