Proof

The sum of the degrees of all the vertices in a graph \(G\), \begin{equation*} \sum_{v\in V(G)} \deg(v), \end{equation*} counts every edge of \(G\) exactly twice.

Thus, \begin{equation*} \sum_{v\in V(G)} \deg(v) = 2 \cdot |E(G)|. \end{equation*}

In particular we see that this sum is even.
