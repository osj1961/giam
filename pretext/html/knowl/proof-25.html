Proof

First we will show that \(A \subseteq B\). Towards that end, suppose \(x \in A\).

\(\vdots\)

Thus \(x \in B\).

Now, we will show that \(B \subseteq A\). Suppose that \(x \in B\).

\(\vdots\)

Thus \(x \in A\).

Therefore \(A \subseteq B \; \land \; B \subseteq A\) so we conclude that \(A=B\).
