Item d.v

If \(a|b\), then \(a\) is a perfect square. If \(a|b\), then \(b\) is a perfect square. Therefore, if \(a|b\), then \(a\) is a perfect square and \(b\) is a perfect square. \hint{Note that the conclusion could be re-expressed as the conjunction of the two conditionals that are found in the premises. This is conjunctive addition with a bit of “window dressing.”}
